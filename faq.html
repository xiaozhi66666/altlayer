FAQ on staking/reALT

  • 1. ELI5 What is ALT staking?

    ALT staking allows ALT holders to stake ALT to earn ALT rewards.

    ALT rewards are given out every second. To withdraw your rewards, you will need to send an on-chain transaction (using the staking portal). This step is only necessary if you do not deposit into the reALT vault.

    When you stake ALT, you subject your stake to a 21-day unbonding period.

  • 2. What is unbonding?

    1.Initiate cooldown (21 days) for the specific amount of ALT

    2.Wait for the cooldown period to be over

    3.Initiate the stake withdrawal

    ALT rewards are given out every second. To withdraw your rewards, you will need to send an on-chain transaction (using the staking portal). This step is only necessary if you do not deposit into the reALT vault. When you stake ALT, you subject your stake to a 21-day unbonding period.

  • 3. ELI5 What is reALT restaking?

    reALT stands for restaked ALT. To get reALT, you can deposit your staked ALT into the reALT vault.

    By depositing your staked ALT into reALT vault, you become eligible to delegate to AltLayer LaunchPools..

    Additionally, reALT will automatically claim ALT staking rewards and deposit the rewards back for staking to get compounded yields for reALT holders. This process is done on a weekly basis.

  • 4. Can I withdraw reALT and is there any unbonding period for reALT?

    There is no unbonding period for reALT token withdrawal. Upon withdrawal from the reALT vault, you will get back your staked ALT instantly.

    The staked ALT can then be redeemed for ALT via the ALT staking contract. This redemption is subject to a 21 days unbonding period.

  • 5. ELI5 What is a LaunchPool?

    LaunchPool allows users to earn LaunchPool project's upcoming tokens by staking specific tokens such as reALT, LaunchPool project's designated token and ETH/LSTs. Delegation of ETH and LSTs is done on the EigenLayer restaking platform.

  • 6. Can I participate in more than one LaunchPool?

    reALT is designed such that your stake can be delegated to multiple LaunchPools at the same time. For example, if you have 10 reALT tokens and you have delegated to LaunchPool X, Y and Z, then, you will have 10 reALT assigned to each of these LaunchPools.

  • 7. What is the LaunchPool APR and when can we get those rewards?

    LaunchPool token rewards will be determined by the project teams and the exact future allocation and distribution timeline will be dependent on them.

  • 8. I am restaking ETH/LSTs with EigenLayer. Why am I not receiving any rewards?

    Rewards for EigenLayer restakers will only be available once EigenLayer releases its payment mechanism on their mainnet.
